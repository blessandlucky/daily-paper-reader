## 摘要
本研究表明，五边形 PdTe2 (penta-PdTe2) 单层是一种高度可调的二维 (2D) 光催化剂，其特征是 1.75 eV 的带隙和高空穴迁移率。利用基于 HSE06 泛函的密度泛函理论计算，我们证明了拉伸应变工程（特别是 +2% 和 +3%）对于实现自发水分解至关重要。在这些应变值下，价带顶和导带底在酸性 (pH=0) 和中性 (pH=7) 条件下均跨越了水的氧化还原电位 (H+/H2 和 O2/H2O)。该单层较低的空穴有效质量促进了电荷的快速提取，减轻了复合，并比许多六边形和五边形对应物更有效地驱动析氧反应 (OER)。吉布斯自由能 (ΔG) 路径表明，析氢反应 (HER) 和 OER 的过电位对机械变形（特别是双轴应变）高度敏感，其中 +3% 的拉伸应变在 pH=7 时实现了 ηHER = 0.70 V 和 ηOER = 0.72 V 的优化过电位平衡。最后，结合光吸收与热力学驱动力，在 pH 7 时实现了 20.40% 的太阳能制氢 (STH) 效率。这超过了此前报道的几种二维催化剂的性能，使 penta-PdTe2 成为可持续太阳能驱动制氢的优选候选材料。

## Abstract
This investigation demonstrates that the pentagonal PdTe2 (penta-PdTe2) monolayer is a highly tunable two-dimensional (2D) photocatalyst, characterized by the bandgap of 1.75 eV and high hole mobility. Using density functional theory calculations with the HSE06 functional, we show that tensile strain engineering (particularly at +2% and +3%) is essential for enabling spontaneous water splitting. At these strain values, the valence-band maximum and conduction-band maximum straddle the water redox potentials (H+/H2 and O2/H2O) in both acidic (pH=0) and neutral (pH=7) conditions. The monolayer's low hole effective mass facilitates rapid charge extraction, mitigating recombination and driving the oxygen evolution reaction (OER) more effectively than many hexagonal and pentagonal counterparts. The Gibbs free energy (ΔG) pathways indicate that overpotentials for the hydrogen evolution reaction (HER) and OER are highly sensitive to mechanical deformation, specifically biaxial strain, through which +3% tensile strain, yielding an optimized balance of overpotentials of ηHER = 0.70 V and ηOER = 0.72 V at pH=7. Finally, integrating optical absorption with thermodynamic driving forces results in a Solar-to-Hydrogen (STH) efficiency of 20.40% at pH 7. This exceeds the performance of several previously reported 2D catalysts, positioning penta-PdTe2 as a superior candidate for sustainable, solar-driven hydrogen production.
