## 摘要
我们考虑 $\mathbb{R}^d$ ($d\ge2$) 中不可压缩 Navier-Stokes 方程的单相自由边界问题。考虑了表面张力的影响。初始区域为气泡外部，是一个外部区域。我们证明了存在一个随该自由边界问题演化的气泡，它在有限时间内塌陷，且其边界的主曲率不会发生爆炸。换言之，在有限时间内形成了所谓的“飞溅奇异性”（splash singularity）。此类结果对于有界初始区域同样成立。为了构建这样一个示例，我们引入了“具有 $\delta$-翼的区域”这一概念，它是一个未嵌入 $\mathbb{R}^d$ 的平坦黎曼流形，但它覆盖了边界发生自交的原始区域的 $\delta$-邻域。

## Abstract
We consider the one-phase free boundary problem for the incompressible Navier-Stokes equations in $\mathbb{R}^d$ ($d\ge2$). The surface tension is taken into account. The initial domain, which is the outside of a bubble, is an exterior domain. We prove that there exists a bubble evolving by this free boundary problem which collapses in a finite time without blowing up of principal curvatures of its boundary. In other words, what is called a splash singularity is formed in a finite time. This type of result is also valid for a bounded initial domain. To construct such an example, we introduce the notion of a domain with $δ$-wing which is a flat Riemannian manifold that is not embedded in $\mathbb{R}^d$, but it covers the $δ$-neighborhood of the original domain whose boundary is self-intersected.
