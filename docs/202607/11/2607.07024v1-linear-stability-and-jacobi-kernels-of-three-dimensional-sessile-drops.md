## 摘要
我们研究了具有自由接触线的三维座滴的线性稳定性。平衡表面是在考虑重力、固定体积和给定接触角的情况下，Young-Laplace 方程的轴对称解。我们推导了重力-毛细能的受约束二阶变分，并构建了相关的雅可比问题。尽管变分稳定性保证了非负性，但由于水平平移保持能量不变，二阶变分必然是退化的。我们的主要结果完全确定了这种退化性。在压力-体积非退化条件 (dV/dλ ≠ 0) 下，我们证明了受约束雅可比算子的核恰好是由水平平移生成的二维空间。证明过程结合了雅可比算子的几何结构与傅里叶模态分析：轴对称模态因压力-体积条件而被排除，第一模态对应平移，而所有高阶模态则通过比较法予以排除。这为模掉平移后的液滴动力学稳定性提供了精确的线性非退化性基础。

## Abstract
We consider the linear stability of three-dimensional sessile drops with a free contact line. The equilibrium surface is an axisymmetric solution of the Young--Laplace equation with gravity, fixed volume, and prescribed contact angle. We derive the constrained second variation of the gravity--capillary energy and formulate the associated Jacobi problem. Although variational stability gives nonnegativity, the second variation is necessarily degenerate because horizontal translations preserve the energy. Our main result identifies this degeneracy completely. Under the pressure--volume nondegeneracy condition $(dV/dλ\neq 0)$, we prove that the kernel of the constrained Jacobi operator is exactly the two-dimensional space generated by horizontal translations. The proof combines the geometric structure of the Jacobi operator with a Fourier-mode analysis: the axisymmetric mode is ruled out by the pressure--volume condition, the first mode gives translations, and all higher modes are excluded by comparison. This provides the precise linear nondegeneracy underlying stability of droplet dynamics modulo translations.
